Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a ticket online?

To purchase a ticket, simply create an account or log in, choose your favorite numbers, and complete your payment securely. Your ticket is stored in your account and can be viewed anytime.

When are Saturday Lotto draws held?

Saturday Lotto draws take place every Saturday evening at 8:30 PM AEST. Results are published shortly after, and you'll be notified if you win.

Can I check past results on your website?

Yes! You can view previous draw results on our Results page. We maintain a history of winning numbers, jackpot amounts, and draw dates so you can stay informed.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We use advanced encryption and data protection protocols to ensure your personal and financial details are 100% secure at all times.

What happens if I win?

If you win a prize, we’ll notify you via email and credit your winnings directly to your account. For major jackpots, our support team will guide you through the secure claim process.
